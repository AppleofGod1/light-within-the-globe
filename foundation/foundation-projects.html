Our Foundation Projects

Light Within the Globe Foundation drives impactful projects around the world — providing education, health, and empowerment where it is needed most.

  • 📚 Education Support — providing books, materials, and school sponsorships for children in underserved communities
  • 🩺 Health & Wellness — organizing health awareness programs, free medical camps, and nutrition support
  • 👩‍👩‍👧‍👦 Women’s Empowerment — training programs for skills development, entrepreneurship, and financial independence
  • 🏫 Community Development — supporting local initiatives that uplift communities and promote positive change
